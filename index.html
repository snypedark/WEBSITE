How We Work

The
process

  1. 01

    Discovery

    Week 1

    We spend time understanding your brand, audience, and what the piece needs to achieve. Briefs, mood boards, reference edits — nothing starts until we're aligned.

  2. 02

    Assembly Edit

    Week 2

    Raw footage becomes a structured narrative. Offline edit with temp grade and music so you can feel the rhythm and pacing before we commit to the fine cut.

  3. 03

    Fine Cut & Refine

    Week 3

    Two rounds of revisions on picture lock. Motion graphics, sound design, and VFX integrated. Every detail scrutinised frame by frame.

  4. 04

    Delivery

    Week 4

    Final color grade, audio mix, and master export in all required formats. Broadcast specs, social cuts, and archival masters — on time, every time.
